The Inspiration

Matatu Culture

Matatu is a Swahili word referring to the public transport vehicles that carry passengers across Kenya. Mathree (Ma3) is the local sheng' word for it. Matatus come in three forms: vans, minibuses, and coaches.

Matatu culture is the unique, over-the-top customisation of these vehicles — bold one-of-a-kind art, neon underglow, massive sound systems, and murals that turn every matatu into a moving statement.

Ma3 Overtake aims to showcase and celebrate this culture on a global stage.
